⚠️ Need Help NOW?

If you are facing immediate eviction, disconnection, or overwhelming debt calls, start here. This is NOT a long-term plan, this is **Triage.**

Debt Collectors Are Calling

You have rights. Don't panic, and don't pay anything until you know the debt is real.

Use this script:

"I am recording this call. I dispute the validity of this debt and request all verification documents be sent to me in writing at [YOUR ADDRESS]. Please stop all phone contact immediately."

**Next Step:** Find a non-profit credit counselor near you.

Find Local Counselor →

Eviction or Foreclosure Notice

Time is critical. Do not ignore papers from the court or your landlord.

Immediate Checklist:

  • **1. Call Legal Aid:** Search for free legal services in your county immediately.
  • **2. Gather Papers:** Find your lease, eviction notice, and court dates.
  • **3. Rental Assistance:** Search for emergency rent relief funds (use the Local Aid Finder).
Find Emergency Rent Aid →

Utility Shut-Off Notice

Call the company *before* the shut-off date. Many providers have emergency programs.

Action Plan:

  • **1. Call Your Provider:** Ask for a payment arrangement or an extension.
  • **2. Check LIHEAP:** This federal program offers energy assistance.
  • **3. Local Charities:** Local churches and non-profits often have utility funds.
Find Utility Assistance →

If you are in immediate danger, dial 911 or your local emergency number.
